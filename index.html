About

Coldflame Studios is a bespoke creative team, based in Melbourne, Australia. Bringing together learning students from information technology and the fine and creative arts, the group is utterly committed to producing projects that explore new ideas and are visually stunning. Through the work of Coldflame Studios, there exists the ideal and vision to find the balance between simple form and rich function – and at the core of the group’s philosophy sits elegance, a descriptor that carries through the entire portfolio.
